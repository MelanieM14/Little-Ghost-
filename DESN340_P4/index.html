About Us

Little Ghost

Welcome to Little Ghost, where every sip, smile, and scented crayon stroke brings warmth to our cozy pop-up cafe! Nestled in the heart of the city, Little Ghost is not just a cafe; it's a whimsical sanctuary where joy and creativity intertwine.

At Little Ghost, we believe in the magic of simple pleasures. Our passion for crafting the perfect cup of coffee is only surpassed by our love for our customers. From the moment you step through our door, you're not just a guest – you're family. We cherish the laughter, the quiet conversations, and the shared moments that make each visit unique.
